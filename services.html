  • Boosts soil microbiome naturally
  • Great for new lawns & revitalization

Lawn Leveling & Yard Grading

Bumpy, uneven yards that collect water and look rough are no match for our leveling service. We topdress and grade your lawn for a smooth, professional surface that drains properly.

  • Professional grading equipment
  • Topsoil & compost mix included
  • Improves drainage significantly

Keep your lawn thick, green, and thriving through every Texas season with our custom organic fertilizer program. No harsh chemicals — safe for kids, pets, and the environment.

  • Kid & pet safe, always

Core Aeration

Relieve soil compaction so water, air, and nutrients can finally reach the root zone. Essential for thick, strong grass in San Antonio's heavy clay soils — best paired with compost topdressing.

  • Professional core aerator machines
  • Best done in spring or fall
  • Pairs perfectly with compost topdressing
  • Visible improvement in weeks
