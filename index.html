What We Do

Our Core Services

From a bare patch of dirt to a thick, healthy lawn — we handle every step of the transformation using organic, eco-friendly methods.

1
Organic Compost Application

Nutrient-rich organic compost enriches your soil, boosting microbial activity and long-term grass health.

2
Lawn Leveling

Fix uneven, bumpy yards that collect water and look rough — we topdress and level for a smooth, uniform surface.

3
Core Aeration

Break up compacted soil so air, water, and nutrients can reach the root zone — essential for thick, green grass.

4
Organic Fertilization

Slow-release, pet-safe fertilizers that feed your lawn gradually all season without burning or chemical runoff.
