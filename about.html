Our Story

Locally Rooted, Organically Driven

Soil Force is a proud San Antonio family business dedicated to providing high-quality, organic lawn care to homeowners in North Spring Branch and surrounding neighborhoods. We started with one simple belief: your lawn should be healthy for your family, not just green on the surface.

With years of hands-on experience in the tough Texas climate, we've developed a deep understanding of San Antonio's soils, its summers, and what it takes to maintain a genuinely thriving lawn here. That's why we focus exclusively on 100% organic solutions — no harsh chemicals, just natural compost, fertilization, and proven techniques that deliver lasting results.

When you call Soil Force, you talk directly to us. No call centers, no middlemen — just an honest conversation about your yard and how we can help.

  • Locally owned & operatedBased in San Antonio — we live and work in the same neighborhoods we serve.
  • 100% organic productsNo synthetic chemicals. Ever. Safe for your kids, your dogs, and the soil microbiome.
  • Direct communicationYou always talk to the owner — we answer fast and give straight, honest answers.
  • Satisfaction guaranteedWe're not done until you're happy. Every single job, every single time.
