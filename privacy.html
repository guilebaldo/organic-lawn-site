Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Home / Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

Soil Force ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have — particularly when you sign up for our email list or request a quote.

Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, request an estimate, or sign up for our email newsletter and offers (such as our $50 discount), we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Property address (if provided, for service estimates)
  • Any details you include in a message to us

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide quotes or estimates
  • To send the discount code you requested and occasional emails about offers, seasonal tips, and updates from Soil Force
  • To improve our services and communications

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Email Marketing & MailerLite

If you opt in to receive emails from us, your information (name and email address) is stored and processed using MailerLite, our email service provider. MailerLite processes data on our behalf in accordance with their own privacy policy and applicable data protection laws.

Every marketing email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time, and we will stop sending you marketing emails — this won't affect any service-related communication you've separately requested.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, and to withdraw consent to marketing communications at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@soil-force.com and we'll respond promptly.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies or local browser storage (for example, to remember that you've already seen a pop-up offer) to improve your browsing experience. These do not track you across other websites.

Children's Privacy

Our services and website are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, reach out to us at hello@soil-force.com or call (726) 234-3062.
